About Shubook Labs

Building Personal Brands Through Powerful Conversations.

Shubook Labs is a premium podcast studio and podcast production company in Jaipur dedicated to helping entrepreneurs, business owners, creators, professionals, and industry leaders build authority through podcasting and content creation.

Founded in December 2025, Shubook Labs was created with a simple vision: To help people share their stories, showcase their expertise, and create meaningful content that builds trust and opportunities.

Meet The Founder

Shubham Saini

Founder, Shubook Labs | Host, The Shubook Show

Shubham Saini is an entrepreneur, content creator, podcast host, and founder of Shubook Labs.

With a background in digital marketing, website development, content creation, podcasting, branding, and media production, he has worked with startups, professionals, creators, and business owners to help them grow their visibility online.

Through The Shubook Show and Shubook Labs, Shubham's mission is to create a platform where meaningful conversations can help people learn, grow, and build stronger personal brands.

Our Story

In today's digital world, attention is one of the most valuable assets.

People don't connect with businesses. People connect with people.

Behind every successful entrepreneur, founder, doctor, coach, consultant, and creator is a story worth sharing.

Shubook Labs was founded to provide a professional platform where these stories can be recorded, documented, and shared with the world.

What started as a passion for content creation and meaningful conversations has evolved into a growing podcast studio and media platform serving ambitious professionals across Jaipur and beyond.

The Shubook Show is the flagship podcast produced by Shubook Labs.
